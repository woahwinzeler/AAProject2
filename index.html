The Wheel is a projection of an image onto a particle map. Using openGL and my knowledge of the graphics pipleline, I used a vertex shader to set the position of the particles in refrence to the rest of the image, allowing for effceient rotation and manipulation of individaul particles. I used a fragment shader to color the particles.
